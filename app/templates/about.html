Project Overview

An AI-driven decision-support system that helps SMEs understand and optimize their operational health.

This is not just a dashboard or reporting tool. It is an intelligent layer that sits on top of existing business signals and provides actionable insights for business improvement.

Financial Analysis

Revenue trends, profit margins, expense tracking

Operational Efficiency

Project success rates, delays, resource utilization

Client Intelligence

Retention rates, satisfaction, acquisition patterns

Risk Detection

Early warning system for business risks
